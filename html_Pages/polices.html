Collection of Personal Data:

During your visit to our website, we will place what is called "cookies." Cookies are text files that some websites you visit deposit on your hard drive, which facilitates your future access to our website and allows you to benefit from the services it offers. These text files contain information that enables the website that deposited them to retrieve it when needed during the user's next visit. The stored information may include:

  1. Saving a security code to identify the user in the system.
  2. Saving page settings.
  3. Saving user type (name and personal email).
  4. Preventing multiple submissions of requests or surveys from the same user.
